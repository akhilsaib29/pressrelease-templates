Rating Rationale

The rating assignment reflects TPL's strong parentage (Tata Group) and its established position in the domestic EPC market. TPL continues to maintain a healthy and diversified order book with strong medium-term revenue visibility.

The rating also factors in TPL's adequate liquidity profile, supported by stable working capital management and financial flexibility associated with the Tata Group. These strengths are partially offset by the inherently working capital-intensive nature of the EPC business and exposure to project execution risks.

Key Strengths

Strong Revenue Visibility

Unexecuted order book of ₹45,000 crores with 3x revenue ratio, diversified across sectors.

Execution Excellence

Proven track record in complex infrastructure projects with focus on technology-intensive work.

Financial Flexibility

Adequate liquidity with ₹1,200 crore unencumbered cash and strong group support.

Challenges & Mitigants

Working Capital Intensity

The EPC business requires substantial working capital due to milestone-based billing and retention money. TPL manages this through back-to-back arrangements with sub-contractors and client advances.

Project Execution Risks

Operations are exposed to typical execution risks and raw material price volatility. Most contracts are protected by price escalation clauses, with adequate financial flexibility to manage impacts.

About Tata Projects Limited

Tata Projects Limited (TPL) is one of the fastest growing infrastructure companies in India, specializing in executing large and complex urban and industrial infrastructure projects.

Business Segments:

  • Urban Infrastructure: Metro rails, airports, smart cities, public spaces
  • Core Infrastructure: Power generation, transmission, roads, bridges
  • Industrial Systems: Hydrocarbon, chemical, metal plant constructions
