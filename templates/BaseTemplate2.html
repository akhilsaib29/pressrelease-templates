Detailed Rationale

The rating upgrade reflects Suzlon Energy Limited's (SEL) consistently improving scale of operations driven by execution of the increasing order book which provides high revenue visibility. The company maintains a strong established market position in the wind turbine generators (WTG) industry while maintaining its healthy financial risk profile.

The group currently holds a cumulative market share of ~32% in the Indian wind turbine segment. The upgrade is further supported by stable cash flows from the Operations and Maintenance (O&M) services business, which manages approximately 15.1 GW of installed fleet in India.

Outlook: Stable

ACER Ratings believes SEL will continue to benefit from its dominant market position and healthy order book. The 'Stable' outlook reflects the expectation that the group will maintain its net-debt free status and improve its operational EBITDA margins over the medium term.

Key Rating Drivers

Healthy Order Book Pipeline

SEL's order book stands at ~5.5 GW (as of May 2025), intended for execution through fiscal 2027. The company has overcome past dependence on customer-backed financing, ensuring smoother execution and growth. The healthy pipeline is supported by India's aggressive renewable energy targets.

Strong Presence in O&M Services

The O&M service business remains a pillar of stability, providing steady recurring revenue. With over 15 GW of capacity under management, the segment acts as a hedge against the cyclicality of the WTG manufacturing business.

Strengthened Financial Profile

Following the successful QIP and debt reduction, SEL has achieved a net cash positive position. Interest coverage has improved to over 7.0x, significantly reducing financial risk.

Liquidity: Strong

Liquidity is supported by a healthy cash and bank balance of Rs. 1,112 Crore as of March 31, 2025. Expected cash accruals of over Rs. 1,000 Crore annually are more than sufficient to cover modest capital expenditure requirements and working capital needs.

About the Company

Suzlon Energy Limited (SEL), founded in 1995, is India's leading wind energy solutions provider. The company offers a comprehensive range of wind turbine generators and associated services. It has a presence across 17 countries and an installed base of over 20 GW worldwide.
