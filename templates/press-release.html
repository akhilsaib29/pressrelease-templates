Credit Rating Action

Press Release for Tata Projects Limited

ACER AA / Stable

ACER

Credit Rating

Brief Summary

ACER has assigned its ratings to the debt instruments of Tata Projects Limited (TPL). The ratings take into account the company's strong market position, established track record in project execution, and stable financial profile. The stable outlook reflects our confidence in the company's ability to maintain its position and financial strength.

Rating Rationale

Reasons for Positive Outlook:

  • Strong market position in infrastructure and construction sector
  • Consistent revenue growth and profitability metrics
  • Diversified project portfolio across sectors
  • Experienced management team with proven track record
  • Strong banking relationships and credit facilities

Analytical Approach

Key Strengths

✓ Market Leadership

Prominent position as a leading EPC contractor in India with a legacy of successful large-scale infrastructure projects.

✓ Financial Stability

Stable operating cash flows and prudent financial management with adequate liquidity position supporting debt servicing.

✓ Project Execution

Excellent track record in timely and within-budget completion of complex infrastructure projects across multiple sectors.

✓ Industry Relationships

Strong relationships with major utilities, government agencies, and private sector clients resulting in steady order inflows.

Areas of Concern

⚠ Sector Dependency

High exposure to infrastructure and utility sector which is cyclical and sensitive to policy changes and government budgets.

⚠ Execution Risks

Projects involve execution risks including cost overruns, delays, and supply chain disruptions in volatile operating environment.

⚠ Working Capital Intensity

Business requires significant working capital management given extended project cycles and payment terms with clients.

⚠ Leverage Profile

Debt levels remain relatively high; continued monitoring of leverage ratios necessary for sustained credit quality.

Liquidity Assessment

Strong Liquidity Position

The company maintains adequate liquidity with a combination of operating cash flows and undrawn bank facilities. Cash and equivalents as of FY 2023-24 stood at INR 180 crores, supplemented by undrawn credit lines of approximately INR 300 crores.

  • Operating Cash Flow: INR 320 Crores (FY 2023-24)
  • Free Cash Flow: INR 150 Crores (after capex)
  • Working Capital Management: Average DPO of 45 days
  • Undrawn Facilities: INR 300 Crores available

Outlook & Sensitivities

Stable Outlook - Rationale

The stable outlook is based on the following expectations:

  • Sustained revenue growth of 8-10% annually driven by order book utilization
  • Maintenance of EBITDA margins in the 13-15% range
  • Net Debt/EBITDA to decline towards 2.0x over the next 12-18 months
  • Interest Coverage to remain above 4.5x

Sensitivity Factors

Rating could be upgraded if:

  • Net Debt/EBITDA sustains below 1.8x
  • EBITDA margins expand to 15%+ levels
  • Order book visibility extends beyond 3 years

Rating could be downgraded if:

  • Net Debt/EBITDA exceeds 3.0x on a sustained basis
  • EBITDA margins compress below 12%
  • Major project delays or significant cost overruns
  • Deterioration in liquidity position
