Brief Summary

Rating Action

ACER has assigned/reaffirmed an AA (Stable) rating to the fund-based and non-fund-based facilities of Tata Projects Limited (TPL). The rating reflects TPL's strong market position, execution capabilities in complex infrastructure projects, healthy financial metrics, and robust parentage support from the Tata Group. The stable outlook indicates our confidence in the company's ability to maintain credit quality over the medium term.

Key Strengths & Weaknesses

STRENGTHS

Strong Market Position

Market leader in infrastructure execution with presence across urban, core, and industrial sectors.

Proven Execution Track Record

Excellent track record in executing large and complex infrastructure projects with timely delivery.

Healthy Financial Metrics

EBITDA margin of 8-9%, improving leverage profile, and strong interest coverage ratios.

Implicit Parental Support

Strong support from Tata Group enhances financial flexibility and market positioning.

WEAKNESSES

Project Execution Risks

Large project exposure carries execution and completion risks, especially in complex urban infrastructure.

Working Capital Intensity

Business model requires substantial working capital management, though this is adequately managed.

Leverage Management

While improving, leverage remains moderate at ~3.0x; tight monitoring required to maintain ratings.

Economic Cycle Dependency

Infrastructure sector growth is inherently dependent on government policies and economic cycles.

Liquidity Assessment

Overall Assessment: Adequate

TPL's liquidity is considered adequate, supported by healthy cash flow from operations, unencumbered cash and bank balances of ~INR 1,200 crore as of December 2025, and high financial flexibility due to its parentage. The average utilization of working capital limits over the last 12 months has been around 75%, providing sufficient headroom. The company has regular access to capital markets and banking facilities for its operational and investment requirements.

Rating Sensitivities

Positive Sensitivities

  • Significant improvement in profitability margins on a sustained basis
  • Substantial reduction in debt levels leading to improvement in debt coverage indicators
  • Improvement in working capital cycle reducing reliance on external debt

Negative Sensitivities

  • Significant delays in project execution impacting revenue booking and profitability
  • Deterioration in working capital management leading to stretched cash flows
  • Large debt-funded capex or investments negatively impacting the financial risk profile

About the Company

Tata Projects Limited (TPL) is one of the fastest growing and most admired infrastructure companies in India. It has expertise in executing large and complex urban and industrial infrastructure projects. TPL operates through its strategically aligned business units: Urban Infrastructure, Core Infra, and Industrial Systems.

Business Unit Specialization
Urban Infrastructure Metro rails, airports, smart cities, and public spaces
Core Infra Power generation, transmission and distribution, roads, and bridges
Industrial Systems Hydrocarbon, chemical, and metal plant constructions
